Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-17

Best Moment FR Publisher is a personal automation tool. It does not collect, sell, or share personal data belonging to any third party.

The only account data it processes is the TikTok access token belonging to its single owner and operator, used exclusively to upload draft posts to that same owner's TikTok account via the official TikTok Content Posting API.

This token is stored securely and privately, is never shared with any third party, and is never used to access any account other than the owner's own.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising technology is used by this tool.

For questions, contact the repository owner via GitHub.
